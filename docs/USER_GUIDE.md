# User Guide: DocuChat AI

Welcome to DocuChat AI! This guide will help you get started with uploading documents and querying them using our AI-powered chat interface.

## 1. Getting Started
When you open the application, you will be assigned a unique **Session ID**. This session keeps your documents private. If you refresh the page or close the browser, your session may expire, and you will need to re-upload your documents.

## 2. Uploading Documents
1. Click the **"Upload"** button or drag and drop your PDF files into the upload zone.
2. You can upload up to **10 PDFs** at a time.
3. **Note**: Only text-based PDFs are supported. Scanned images or handwritten notes (OCR) are not supported in this version.

## 3. Chatting with your Data
Once the status indicator shows that your files have been processed:
1. Type your question in the chat box (e.g., "What is the budget mentioned in the financial report?").
2. The AI will search through all your uploaded documents simultaneously.
3. If the information is not in your documents, the AI will tell you: *"The answer was not found in the uploaded documents."*

## 4. Understanding Citations
Every answer provided by the AI includes **Citations**. 
- Look for the file name and page number below the AI's response.
- These citations point directly to the source of the information so you can verify the accuracy yourself.

## 5. Managing Files
- You can view the list of currently active files in the sidebar.
- To remove a document from the AI's memory, click the **Delete** (trash can) icon next to the file name.

## 6. Tips for Better Results
- **Be Specific**: Instead of "Tell me about the contract," try "What are the payment terms in the Service Agreement?"
- **Page References**: If a document is very long, the AI will prioritize the most relevant sections.
- **Session Limits**: Remember that this is a stateless application; your chat history and files are cleared once the session ends.
