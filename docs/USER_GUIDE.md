# DocuChat AI User Guide

Welcome to **DocuChat AI**! This guide will help you understand how to use the platform to interact with your PDF documents effectively.

## 1. Getting Started
DocuChat AI allows you to "talk" to your documents. Instead of searching through hundreds of pages, you can simply ask questions and get answers backed by citations.

## 2. Uploading Documents
- **Supported Formats:** Only text-based PDF files are supported. Scanned images (OCR) are not currently supported.
- **Limits:** You can upload up to **10 documents** per session.
- **How to:** Use the upload interface to select your files. Once uploaded, the system takes a few seconds to "read" and index them.

## 3. Asking Questions
When asking questions, keep these tips in mind:
- **Be Specific:** Instead of "Tell me about money," try "What was the net profit in Q3?"
- **Context is Key:** The AI only knows what is in the documents you uploaded. It will not use outside knowledge.
- **Follow-up:** You can ask follow-up questions. The AI remembers the context of your current conversation.

## 4. Understanding Responses
Every response from DocuChat AI consists of two parts:
1.  **The Answer:** A natural language response generated based *only* on your files.
2.  **Sources/Citations:** A list of exactly which document and which page the information came from. 

*Note: If the information is not in your documents, the AI will tell you: "The answer was not found in the uploaded documents."*

## 5. Privacy and Sessions
- **Isolation:** Your documents are private to your session. Other users cannot see or query your data.
- **Session Lifecycle:** Once you close your browser or the session times out, your documents and the conversation history are deleted from our active memory.

## 6. Troubleshooting
- **"Answer not found":** Ensure the information exists in the PDF. If the PDF is a scanned image, the AI cannot read the text.
- **Upload Errors:** Ensure your files are under the size limit and are valid PDF formats.
- **Slow Responses:** Large documents may take a few extra seconds to process during the initial upload.
